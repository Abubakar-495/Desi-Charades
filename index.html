How It Works

Desi Charades is easy to play in just 3 steps. Jump in with friends or family and enjoy endless fun, laughter, and crazy moments!

Choose Category

Select from various desi categories like Bollywood, Cricket, Food, Festivals, and more.

Guess the Word

Use gestures, expressions, and movements to act out the word without speaking.

Tilt & Score

Your team guesses correctly to earn points. Tilt your phone to control the timer!
